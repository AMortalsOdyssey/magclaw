Privacy Policy

This policy is a built-in first version for MagClaw workspaces. MagClaw stores the account, role, session, invitation, reset, message, task, agent, and runtime metadata needed to operate your workspace.

Data We Use

Your workspace may store your email address, display name, avatar, role, last activity time, login sessions, invitation status, password hash, and password reset status. Passwords are never stored in plain text.

Workspace Control

Your administrator controls workspace membership and may revoke sessions, reset passwords, or remove access. Local deployments may keep files, attachments, and runtime data on the host machine configured by the workspace owner.

Retention

Workspace data is retained according to the deployment owner's operational needs. Contact your administrator for account access, correction, deletion, or export requests.
