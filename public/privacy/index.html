Privacy Policy

This policy is the built-in starter policy for your MagClaw workspace. To run your workspace, MagClaw stores the metadata needed for accounts, roles, sessions, invitations, resets, messages, tasks, agents, and runtime operations.

Data We Use

Your workspace may store email addresses, display names, avatars, roles, last activity times, login sessions, invitation status, password hashes, and password reset state. Passwords are never stored in plaintext.

Workspace Control

Your administrator controls workspace membership and may revoke sessions, reset passwords, or remove access. Local deployments may store files, attachments, and runtime data on hosts configured by the workspace owner.

Retention

Workspace data is retained according to the operational needs of the deployment owner. For account access, correction, deletion, or export requests, contact your administrator.

隐私政策

本政策是 MagClaw 工作区内置的初始版本。为了运行你的工作区，MagClaw 会存储账户、角色、会话、邀请、重置、消息、任务、智能体和运行时所需的元数据。

我们使用的数据

你的工作区可能会存储邮箱地址、显示名称、头像、角色、上次活动时间、登录会话、邀请状态、密码哈希和密码重置状态。密码绝不会以明文存储。

工作区控制

你的管理员控制工作区成员资格，并可以撤销会话、重置密码或移除访问权限。本地部署可能会把文件、附件和运行时数据保存在工作区所有者配置的主机上。

保留期限

工作区数据会根据部署所有者的运营需要保留。如需账户访问、更正、删除或导出，请联系你的管理员。
